This guide is for f2p and low spender perspective, if you are whale or kraken, can replace normal heroes with your star heroes!

Whats considered early, mid, and late game ?
Early = 1 team
Mid = 2-3 teans
Late = 4-5 teams

Some star heroes that are good at base copy for example :
Nuwa, Amun ra, Nyx, Dio

Star heroes that needs heavy investment : 
Zeus and Nezha

Poseidon, Momus, Jormu, Hlad
Prometheus, Khepri, Set

Prioritize build these damage dealer (dd) from the beginning, DD usually from archer, assassin, and mage. Warrior such as nezha and hlad can be dd too
isis, hecate, nemesis, hlad
jingwei, bastet, anubis, phoenix
Hela
